Refund Policy

Summary: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. After 7 days, all payments are non-refundable.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are a first-time subscriber and are not satisfied with Super AI Review, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial payment.

Eligibility:

  • Only applies to first-time subscribers
  • Request must be made within 7 days of payment
  • Account must not have been used for fraudulent activities

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following cases:

  • After the 7-day guarantee period has expired
  • For renewal payments (monthly, annual, or multi-year)
  • For partial months or unused portions of subscriptions
  • If your account was terminated due to Terms of Service violations
  • For add-on services or one-time fees
  • If you received a discounted or promotional rate

3. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Upon cancellation:

  • You will retain access until the end of your current billing period
  • No refund will be provided for the remaining time
  • Auto-renewal will be disabled
  • You can export your data before the subscription ends

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the 7-day guarantee period:

  1. Email us at refunds@superaireview.com
  2. Include your account email and reason for refund
  3. We will process your request within 3-5 business days
  4. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method

5. Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds typically take:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 3-7 business days
  • Wallets: 1-3 business days

Processing times depend on your bank or payment provider.

6. Chargebacks

If you file a chargeback or payment dispute without first contacting us, your account will be immediately suspended and may be permanently terminated.

7. Service Credits

In case of service outages or technical issues, we may offer service credits (extended subscription time) instead of refunds at our discretion.
