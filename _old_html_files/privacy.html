Customer Review Data:

  • Service selections and ratings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Generate AI-powered reviews using OpenAI
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send service notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve the Service
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data with:

  • OpenAI: Review content is sent to OpenAI's API to generate AI-written reviews

  • Analytics Providers: Anonymized usage data for service improvement

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account cancellation:

  • Account data is retained for 90 days for recovery purposes
  • Financial records are retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@superaireview.com

6. Data Security

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
