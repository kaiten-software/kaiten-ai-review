üì± QR Codes & Review Collection

How do I get my QR code? +
After onboarding, go to your dashboard and click "Download QR Code". You can print it or display it digitally at your business location.
Where should I place the QR code? +
Best locations: Reception desk, checkout counter, table tents, receipts, business cards, or near the exit. Make sure it's visible and accessible to customers.
Can I have multiple QR codes for different locations? +
Yes! With multi-location support, you can create separate QR codes for each location and track reviews separately. This feature is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.
What happens when a customer scans the QR code? +
They're taken to a mobile-friendly review page showing your branding. They select the service received, quality attributes, how it made them feel, and which staff member served them. Our AI then generates a professional review.

ü§ñ AI Review Generation

How does AI review generation work? +
When a customer submits their feedback, we send their selections (service, quality, feelings, staff) to ChatGPT, which generates a natural, authentic-sounding review. The customer can then copy and post it to Google.
Are AI-generated reviews compliant with Google's policies? +
Yes, when used correctly. The reviews are based on genuine customer experiences. The AI simply helps customers articulate their thoughts better. However, you must ensure reviews come from real customers who actually used your services.
What if a customer gives a low rating? +
If a customer selects less than 3 stars, we DON'T generate a public review. Instead, we send the feedback privately to you via email so you can address the issue internally. This protects your online reputation.
Can customers edit the AI-generated review? +
Yes! Customers can add their own comments during the review process, and they can edit the AI-generated text before posting to Google.
