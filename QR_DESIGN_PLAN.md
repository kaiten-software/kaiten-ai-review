# QR Stand Design Transformation Plan

## Objective
Transform all 5 QR stand designs from abstract 3D mockups to Paytm-style corporate designs with:
- **KAITEN AI REVIEW** branding (not "Super AI Review")
- Proper boundaries (nothing cut off)
- Corporate color schemes
- Professional layout matching Paytm reference

## Paytm Reference Structure
1. **Top Section** (80px height): Colored background with brand name
   - "KAITEN" (large, bold, white)
   - "AI REVIEW" (small, subtitle)
   
2. **Middle Section** (flexible): White background
   - Heading: "Review Us Here" / "Share Feedback" / etc.
   - QR Code (132x132px) with border
   - Business logo overlay (centered on QR)
   - Business name below QR
   
3. **Bottom Section** (60px height): Dark footer
   - Icons + text (Rate • Review • Share)
   - OR step indicators
   - OR "Powered by Kaiten AI"
